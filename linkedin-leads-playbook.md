## Outreach scripts (use exactly, edit lightly)

### Template A — Cold connection request (send to 2nd-degree fresh leads)

> Hi [First name], I noticed we're both connected to [mutual name]. I run Paid Media for international startups entering France — saw your role at [company] and figured we'd swap notes some day. Open to connecting.

**Why this works:**
- Mentions mutual connection (warm signal)
- States your positioning in 11 words
- Low-pressure CTA ("swap notes some day")
- No sales pitch — that comes later

**Character limit: 300 chars on free LinkedIn.** Above is 235 chars, fits with room.

---

### Template B — DM to existing 1st-degree connection (Bryan Kulka style)

> Hey Bryan — quick one. I'm Moustafa, Google & Meta Ads strategist for FR market entry (€10–100K monthly budgets, 47× peak ROAS).
>
> Just shipped 3 playbooks at bahri.studio/insights — the ROAS benchmarks one and the Conversions API stack one might be relevant to what you're working on at [his company].
>
> Open to a 15-min call if you ever want a second opinion on a Paid Media setup. Or just curious: what's the biggest acquisition lever you're testing right now?

**Why this works:**
- Establishes credibility in 1 line (47× ROAS)
- Drops value (3 free playbooks) before any ask
- Open-ended question = conversation, not pitch
- Calendly link is implied, not spammed

### Template C — Pending request follow-up (someone hasn't accepted yet)

> Hi [First name] — sent a connection request a few days ago, just adding context.
>
> I help [their company type — e.g. "B2C ecommerce brands" or "B2B SaaS startups"] with Paid Media for the French market. 9 yrs running Google + Meta Ads, 47× peak ROAS on Search High-Intent.
>
> If French Paid Media isn't on your radar right now, totally fine to ignore. If it is, I just published 3 playbooks at bahri.studio/insights that might save you a few weeks: the France market entry one, the Conversions API stack, and ROAS benchmarks by vertical.
>
> Either way, hope the [month/season] is going well.
> Moustafa

**Why this works:**
- Acknowledges the pending state (no awkwardness)
- Says "ignore if not relevant" — removes pressure
- Drops 3 specific value props (the articles)
- Doesn't ask for time; lets them come to you

**Send via:** click their name → Message (works even before they accept the connection in some cases) or comment on a recent post of theirs first to warm up.

---

### Template D — Engagement-first warm-up (highest reply rate)

For the leads where direct DM feels cold, do this first:

1. Find their last 3 posts
2. Drop a substantive comment (3+ sentences) on one of them
3. Wait 24-48 hours
4. Send connection request with note: "Followed your posts on [topic]. Would love to connect — I work on [your stuff]."

This converts at 3-5× the rate of cold connection requests.

## Outreach cadence (do not skip)

**Daily target:** 10 connection requests + 3 DMs to 1st-degrees + 5 substantive post comments.

**Day 1:** Send connection (Template A) or comment on their post.
**Day 4-7:** If accepted, send Template B style intro DM.
**Day 14:** Soft nudge — share a relevant article or insight, no ask.
**Day 30:** Direct ask — "Open to a 15-min screen-share on your account?" with calendar link.
**Day 60:** If silent, send last touchpoint with the FAQ link. Then drop them.

**Weekly:** post 1 piece of content yourself (you already do this). Comment on 5 prospect posts.

**LinkedIn limits (free tier):**
- ~100 connection requests per week
- Don't spam — LinkedIn restricts accounts that send too many ignored requests
- Withdraw old pending requests every Friday (5+ days unaccepted = withdraw)

## Lead scoring framework

Before sending any outreach, rate the lead 1-5 on these:

1. **Title fit** (5 = exact ICP / decision-maker, 1 = adjacent)
2. **Company stage** (5 = Seed-Series B, 1 = enterprise)
3. **Geography** (5 = Paris with international parent, 1 = Paris-only no growth ambition)
4. **Mutual connections** (5 = strong mutual, 1 = none)
5. **Activity** (5 = posts about Paid Media weekly, 1 = inactive 6+ mo)

**Total 20+ = Tier 1 outreach** (DM script + personalized angle)
**15-19 = Tier 2 outreach** (template A connection, follow up if accepted)
**Under 15 = skip**

## What NOT to do

- Don't send the same template to everyone. 60-second personalization on each beats spam.
- Don't lead with "I'd love to introduce my services." Lead with value or curiosity.
- Don't pitch your services in the first message. Earn the right with a connection + 1 value drop first.
- Don't connect and immediately DM — wait 48h, drops conversion 30%.
- Don't ignore mutual connections. "We're both connected to X" is your single strongest opener.
- Don't outreach without first having something to point to (your bahri.studio is now that thing — every cold DM should reference one specific page).
- Don't outreach Mondays before 10 AM or Fridays after 4 PM. Tue-Thu mornings convert best.
- Don't expect responses from cold connections. 10-20% accept rate is normal. Of accepts, ~5-10% reply to DMs.
